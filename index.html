Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about PetPal BioTag

How does PetPal BioTag monitor my pet's health?

PetPal BioTag uses advanced sensors to monitor heart rate, respiratory rate, temperature, and hydration levels in real-time. The data is analyzed by our AI system to detect anomalies and potential health issues.

Is PetPal BioTag comfortable for my senior pet?

Yes! PetPal BioTag is lightweight, non-invasive, and specifically designed for senior pets. Most pets don't even notice they're wearing it.

How long does the battery last?

PetPal BioTag has a battery life of up to 3 weeks on a single charge, depending on usage and settings.

Can I share the data with my veterinarian?

Absolutely! PetPal BioTag generates detailed health reports that you can easily share with your veterinarian to provide a comprehensive view of your pet's health between visits.
